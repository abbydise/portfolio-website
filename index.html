About Me!

Hello, my name is Abigail Dise. I am a pre-junior at Drexel University in Philadelphia, PA. My major is in Computer Science and my minor is in Computer Engineering. I have a background in Computer Information Systems, but my primary focus is software development.

I began my programming journey in middle school where I first started learning Python through a mobile app on my phone. I have since learned multiple languages through various platforms. I have the most experience in Python, HTML & CSS, and JavaScript. The tech stack I am most familiar with for web development is the MERN stack (MongoDB, Express, React, Node.js).

I am more than willing and capable to learn what I need to for any position. I am a quick and active learner as I love to learn new things constantly. I will put in the effort to be the employee a company needs.

My goal for this website is to showcase my skills and create a central point for the information necessary for potential employers or future collaborators to learn a bit more about me.
