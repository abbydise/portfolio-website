About Me!

Hello, my name is Abigail Dise. I am a pre-junior at Drexel University in Philadelphia, PA. My major is in Computer Science and my minor is in Computer Engineering. I have a background in Computer Information Systems, but my primary focus is software development.

My main skills are in Python and web development, but I am willing and capable of learning new technologies. I am currently in the process of learning React Native and Spring.

I began my programming journey in middle school where I first started learning Python through a mobile app on my phone. As I grew to love coding more, I subscribed to an online service called Codecademy which provided me with a more well-rounded instruction in various programming languages. I have since learned Java, Javascript, HTML & CSS (obviously), and SQL.

My goal for this website is to showcase my skills and create a central point for the information necessary for potential employers or future collaborators to learn a bit more about me. There are 3 main sections to my website -- Education, Experience, and Projects. Please consider viewing each page as they provide more details. My contact information is in the footer of each page if you wish to contact me as well!
